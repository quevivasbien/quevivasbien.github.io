About

I'm Mckay Jensen -- this is my site for posting some of my writing and updates on my projects.

"Que vivas bien" means, roughly, "may you live well" in Spanish. A lot of my writing is related to my thinking about what it means to live well and what we can do to help ourselves and others to live well. You can also take the title of this site as a friendly wish from me to you: "May you live well!"

Feel free to contact me or check out my work elsewhere using the links at the bottom of the page.
